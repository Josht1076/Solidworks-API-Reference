Remarks

To access a CalloutAngleVariable object:

  1. Call IDisplayDimension::GetHoleCalloutVariables.
  2. Iterate through the CalloutVariable objects returned by IDisplayDimension::GetHoleCalloutVariables and call ICalloutVariable::Type.

If the type of hole callout equals swCalloutVariableType_e.swCalloutVariable_Angle, then that object is a CalloutAngleVariable object.
