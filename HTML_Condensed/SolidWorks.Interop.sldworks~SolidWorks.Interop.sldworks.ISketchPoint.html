Remarks

The ISketchPoint object provides access to sketch points while the ISketchSegment object provides access to sketch arcs, lines, ellipses, parabolas, and splines and their corresponding ISketchArc, ISketchEllipse, ISketchLine, ISketchParabola, ISketchText, and ISketchSpline interfaces.
