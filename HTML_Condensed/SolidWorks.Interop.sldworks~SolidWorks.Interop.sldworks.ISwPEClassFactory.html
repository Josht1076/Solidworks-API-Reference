Remarks

This interface provides a callback mechanism in which:

  1. SOLIDWORKS requests the partner's key through the add-in by calling ISwPEManager::IdentifyToSW,
  2. The partner add-in sends a license key back to SOLIDWORKS in ISwPEClassFactory::SetPartnerKey, and
  3. SOLIDWORKS uses the license key to verify the entitlement of the SOLIDWORKS Partner and returns ISwPEToken.

 See SOLIDWORKS Partner Program.
